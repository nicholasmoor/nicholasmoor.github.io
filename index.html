Services

I provide an extensive range of services to my patients. See what sets me apart.

Book appointment
  • Dermatology 
    I provide the diagnosis and management of common skin conditions such as acne, eczema, psoriasis, skin cancer, and many more. I help provide preventive measures to help you maintain healthy skin, such as sun protection and early detection of skin abnormalities. Book here.
  • Sports medicine
    Whether you are a professional athlete, weekend warrior, or someone who simply enjoys regular exercise, I will help you keep in top form. Book here.
  • Travel medicine, including yellow fever vaccination
    I offer personalized recommendations based on the specific destination, type of travel, and your medical history. My aim is to ensure that you are well prepared for your trip and can enjoy your travel experience with peace of mind knowing that your health is well-protected. Book here.
  • Sexual health, including HIV PrEP
    I work closely with you to assess your individual risk factors and provide you with personalized advice and support to help you make informed decisions about your sexual health. Book here.
  • General consultations
    I provide comprehensive general consultations where you can discuss any health concerns you may have. I take the time to listen to you and understand your concerns, and I work with you to develop a personalized treatment plan that addresses your individual needs. Book here.
  • Chronic disease management
    I specialise in managing chronic conditions such as diabetes, asthma, and heart disease, among others. I provide ongoing support to help you manage your condition and prevent complications. Book here.
  • Preventative health
    You can have your regular check-ups, vaccinations, and health screenings to detect potential health problems early and prevent future illness with me. Book here.
  • Women's health
    I offer a range of women's health services, including cervical cancer screening tests, breast cancer screening, and contraception advice. Book here.
  • Men's health
    I provide a range of men's health services, including prostate checks, testicular cancer screening, and advice on sexual health. Book here.
  • Children's health
    I am experienced in providing a range of children's health services, including vaccinations, developmental checks, and treatment of childhood illnesses. Book here.
  • Minor procedures
    I am skilled in performing minor procedures, such as suturing of wounds, removal of moles, skin cancer excisions, and skin biopsies. Book here.
  • Health assessments
    I provide health assessments for patients over the age of 75 or for patients at risk of a chronic medical condition to identify potential health risks and develop a treatment plan. Book here.
  • Diabetes
    From regular monitoring to medication management and lifestyle changes, I am here to help you manage your diabetes and live your best life. Book here.
  • Asthma
    My approach to asthma care is centered on empowering you with the knowledge and skills to manage your asthma effectively, reduce the risk of exacerbations, and improve your quality of life. Book here.
