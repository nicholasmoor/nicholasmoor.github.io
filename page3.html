Hello, I'm Dr Nicholas Moor, a general practitioner who is excited to be serving the community across two convenient locations in Epping and Carlingford. My patients can visit me at either location, thanks to the shared patient files system that I have in place. This means that my patients can see me at the location that is most convenient for them without worrying about transferring their medical records.

As a dedicated and caring doctor, I am passionate about providing the highest quality healthcare to my patients. I take the time to listen to their concerns and work closely with them to develop personalized treatment plans that address their specific needs. My goal is to help my patients achieve optimal health and wellness, and I strive to create a welcoming and comfortable environment where they can feel relaxed and confident about their healthcare.

I am excited to meet and get to know you at both of my clinic locations in Epping and Carlingford. Whether you are seeking preventative care, treatment for an illness, or medical advice for travel, I am here to provide you with the care and support that you need. I look forward to welcoming you to my practice and working together to achieve your health goals.
