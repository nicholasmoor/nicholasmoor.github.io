Sexual Health

Confidential, Inclusive, Expert Care for Your Sexual Health that Supports You with Pride and Respect

HIV Infections

Dr Nicholas Moor is a warm, inclusive, and highly experienced GP who is also an HIV S100 authorised prescriber. He offers personalised, evidence-based HIV care in a safe and welcoming space, with a strong focus on supporting the LGBTQ+ community. Whether you’re newly diagnosed or have been living with HIV for years, Dr Moor is here to walk alongside you - offering up-to-date treatment options, including same-day ART initiation, regular check-ups, and ongoing support for your overall health and wellbeing.

You’ll never feel like just a number in Dr Nicholas Moor’s care. He takes the time to listen, to understand your story, and to make sure your treatment fits your life - not the other way around. With a friendly, non-judgemental approach, he’s committed to building trust and helping you feel confident in managing your health. If you're looking for respectful, expert HIV care from someone who truly gets it, book an appointment with Dr Nicholas Moor today.

HIV PrEP

Offering supportive, stigma-free access to HIV PrEP (pre-exposure prophylaxis), Dr Nicholas Moor provides a warm, approachable PrEP service that puts your comfort and wellbeing first. Whether you're thinking about starting PrEP or already taking it, he’ll take the time to listen, answer your questions, and make sure you feel fully supported every step of the way. With a strong background in LGBTQ+ healthcare, Dr Nicholas Moor understands the unique needs of the community and provides a safe, judgment-free space where you can talk openly about your health and sexual wellbeing.

There’s no pressure and no assumptions - just friendly, expert care to help you stay in control of your health. From starting PrEP to managing regular check-ups and testing, Dr Nicholas Moor will work with you to make the process simple and stress-free. If you're looking for a GP who genuinely understands and cares, book an appointment with Dr Nicholas Moor today.

Chlamydia and Gonorrhoea

Dr Nicholas Moor offers friendly, down-to-earth care for anyone needing testing or treatment for chlamydia and gonorrhoea. He knows that talking about STIs can feel awkward or even a bit scary — but it really doesn’t have to be. With his warm, non-judgemental approach, you’ll feel listened to and respected, no matter your situation. Whether you’re experiencing symptoms, have been told a partner has tested positive, or just want a routine check-up, Dr Moor will make the process simple and supportive.

Appointments are relaxed and private, with clear explanations and no shame - just the care you need to look after your health. As someone who’s genuinely committed to LGBTQ+ inclusive healthcare, Dr Moor creates a space where you can be yourself and feel safe. If you're due for a test or just want to chat about your sexual health, book an appointment today.
