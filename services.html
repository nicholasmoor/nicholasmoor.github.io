Services
As your local general practitioner in Epping, Carlingford and Parramatta, Dr Nicholas Moor provides a range of medical services to his patients, including:

  • General Consultations
    Dr Nicholas Moor offers comprehensive general consultations where patients can discuss any health concerns they may have. During the consultation, he will conduct a thorough physical examination, review the patient's medical history, and provide a diagnosis or refer to a specialist if necessary. Dr Nicholas Moor takes the time to listen to his patients and understand their concerns, and he works with them to develop a personalized treatment plan that addresses their individual needs. Book here.
  • Chronic Disease Management
    Dr Nicholas Moor specializes in managing chronic conditions such as diabetes, asthma, and heart disease. He works closely with his patients to develop an individualized management plan that includes lifestyle changes, medication management, and regular monitoring. Dr Nicholas Moor provides ongoing support to his patients to help them manage their condition and prevent complications. Book here.
  • Preventative Health
    Dr Nicholas Moor is committed to helping his patients maintain good health and prevent illness. He offers regular check-ups, vaccinations, and health screenings to detect potential health problems early and prevent future illness. Dr Nicholas Moor works with his patients to develop a personalized preventative health plan that addresses their individual risk factors and health needs. Book here.
  • Mental Health Care
    Dr Nicholas Moor is experienced in providing mental health care services, including counseling and medication management for patients experiencing depression, anxiety, or other mental health conditions. He provides a safe and supportive environment for his patients to discuss their concerns and works with them to develop a personalized treatment plan that addresses their mental health needs. Book here.
  • Women's Health
    Dr Nicholas Moor offers a range of women's health services, including cervical screening tests, breast cancer screening, and contraception advice. He works closely with his female patients to address their unique health needs and provide them with the care and support they need to maintain good health. Book here.
  • Men's Health
    Dr Nicholas Moor provides a range of men's health services, including prostate checks, testicular cancer screening, and advice on sexual health. He works with his male patients to address their specific health concerns and provide them with the care and support they need to maintain good health. Book here.
  • Children's Health
    Dr Nicholas Moor is experienced in providing a range of children's health services, including vaccinations, developmental checks, and treatment of childhood illnesses. He works closely with parents to ensure that their children receive the care and support they need to grow and develop healthily. Book here.
  • Minor Procedures
    Dr Nicholas Moor is skilled in performing minor procedures, such as suturing of wounds, removal of moles, and skin biopsies. He provides a safe and comfortable environment for his patients and ensures that they receive the highest quality care during their procedure. Book here.
  • Health Assessments
    Dr Nicholas Moor provides health assessments for patients over the age of 75 or for patients at risk of a chronic medical condition to identify potential health risks and develop a treatment plan. He works with his patients to ensure that they receive the care and support they need to maintain good health as they age. Book here.
  • Sexual Health, including HIV PrEP
    Dr Nicholas Moor provides comprehensive sexual health services, including advice and support on HIV PrEP (pre-exposure prophylaxis), a medication taken to reduce the risk of HIV transmission in people who are at high risk. He works closely with his patients to assess their individual risk factors and provide them with personalized advice and support to help them make informed decisions about their sexual health. Dr Nicholas Moor also offers testing and treatment for sexually transmitted infections (STIs), as well as contraception advice and support. He provides a safe and supportive environment for his patients to discuss their sexual health concerns and works with them to develop a personalized treatment plan that meets their unique needs. Book here.
  • Travel Medicine
    Dr Nicholas Moor has a particular interest in travel medicine and is experienced in providing comprehensive medical advice to patients travelling overseas. He is an accredited yellow fever vaccination provider. His travel medicine services include advice on travel vaccinations, preventative medications, and general health advice for travellers. Dr Moor provides up-to-date information on travel health risks, including infectious diseases and how to prevent them. He also offers personalized recommendations based on the specific destination, type of travel, and the patient's medical history. Dr Moor's aim is to ensure that his patients are well prepared for their trip and can enjoy their travel experience with peace of mind knowing that their health is well-protected. Book here.
  • Dermatology
    Dr Nicholas Moor has a special interest in dermatology and is able to provide expert diagnosis and treatment for a wide range of skin conditions. He is experienced in the diagnosis and management of conditions such as acne, eczema, psoriasis, skin cancer, and many others. Book here.
  • Sports Medicine
    As a general practitioner with a special interest in sports medicine, Dr Nicholas Moor is dedicated to helping athletes of all levels achieve their goals while minimizing their risk of injury. Whether the patient is a professional athlete, weekend warrior, or someone who simply enjoys regular exercise, Dr Moor offers a range of services to help keep them in top form. These may include pre-participation physical exams, injury assessments and management, rehabilitation programs, and advice on injury prevention and nutrition. Dr Moor is passionate about helping patients achieve their sporting goals and lead a healthy, active lifestyle. Book here.
  • Diabetes
    Dr Nicholas Moor offers a comprehensive diabetes service that includes initial screening, ongoing management, and education on lifestyle changes and medication options for diabetes. He works closely with patients to develop personalized treatment plans that prioritize their individual needs and goals. Dr Moor emphasizes the importance of regular monitoring and communication with patients to ensure optimal health outcomes. From regular monitoring to medication management and lifestyle changes, Dr Moor is here to help you manage your diabetes and live your best life. Book here.
  • Asthma
    Dr Nicholas Moor provides a comprehensive asthma service that involves the assessment, diagnosis, and management of asthma. He has extensive experience in working with patients of all ages and backgrounds. His approach to asthma care is centered on empowering patients with the knowledge and skills to manage their condition effectively, reduce the risk of exacerbations, and improve their quality of life. Book here.
