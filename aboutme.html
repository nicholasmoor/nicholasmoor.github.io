About me

I am Dr Nicholas Moor, a highly skilled and compassionate doctor with extensive experience in providing top-quality medical care to patients. I completed my medical degree at the University of Newcastle and have since gone on to complete a Master of Public Health at Griffith University. I am also a proud Fellow of the Royal Australian College of General Practitioners, which is a testament to my commitment to excellence in the field of medicine.

Fluent in both English and German, I am able to provide medical care to a diverse range of patients from different backgrounds. I am an active member of the community and enjoy volunteering my time with St John Ambulance to provide first aid services at local events. This allows me to give back to the community and gain valuable experience in emergency medicine.

My areas of special interest include preventative medicine, dermatology, and travel medicine. I am passionate about promoting good health and wellness and helping my patients maintain optimal health through education, screening, and early detection of illness. I am also experienced in diagnosing and treating a wide range of dermatological conditions, including skin cancers, and have a keen interest in travel medicine, ensuring that my patients stay healthy while travelling overseas.

Further interests include sports medicine and sexual health including HIV PrEP. As a general practitioner, I have a particular interest in sexual health and the role of preventative medicine in this area, including HIV PrEP. I am dedicated to providing my patients with comprehensive sexual health services and advice, including testing and treatment for sexually transmitted infections, contraceptive advice, and counselling on safe sex practices, including the safe use of HIV PrEP. With my interest in sports medicine, I understand the importance of maintaining a healthy and active lifestyle, especially for those who engage in physical activities such as sports or exercise. I offer a range of services related to sports medicine, including injury management and prevention, and rehabilitation and performance enhancement. 

I believe that building strong relationships with my patients is essential for providing effective medical care, and I am dedicated to listening to their concerns and answering their questions to develop personalized treatment plans. My ultimate goal is to help my patients achieve optimal health and well-being and provide them with the highest level of care possible.
