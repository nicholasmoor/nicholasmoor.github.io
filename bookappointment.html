Hello, I'm Dr Nicholas Moor, a general practitioner who is excited to be serving the community across three convenient locations in Epping, Carlingford and Parramatta. My patients can visit me at any location, but thanks to the shared patient files system that I have in place my patients can see me at Epping or Carlingford without worrying about transferring their medical records.

As a dedicated and caring doctor, I am passionate about providing the highest quality healthcare to my patients. I take the time to listen to their concerns and work closely with them to develop personalized treatment plans that address their specific needs. My goal is to help my patients achieve optimal health and wellness, and I strive to create a welcoming and comfortable environment where they can feel relaxed and confident about their healthcare.

I am excited to meet and get to know you at any of my clinic locations in Epping, Carlingford and Parramatta. Whether you are seeking preventative care, treatment for an illness, or medical advice for travel, I am here to provide you with the care and support that you need. I look forward to welcoming you to my practice and working together to achieve your health goals.
